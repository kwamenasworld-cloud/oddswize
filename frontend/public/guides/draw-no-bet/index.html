How draw no bet works

You choose the home or away team to win.

If your team wins, the bet pays. If the match is a draw, your stake is returned.

When to use it

  • Tight matches where a draw feels likely.
  • Backing a slight favorite but limiting draw risk.
  • Building safer accumulator selections.

FAQ

What happens if the match is a draw?

Your stake is returned. The bet is settled as a push.

Is draw no bet the same as Asian handicap 0?

Yes. Draw no bet is equivalent to Asian handicap 0.

Is the payout lower than 1X2?

Usually yes, because you remove the draw outcome and reduce risk.
