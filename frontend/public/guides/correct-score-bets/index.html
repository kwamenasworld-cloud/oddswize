Correct score betting focuses on the exact final score, which makes odds higher and riskier.

What is a correct score bet?

You predict the exact final score, such as 1-0 or 2-1.

Only the exact score wins, so outcomes are lower probability.

How to approach it

  • Focus on 2-3 likely scorelines instead of many.
  • Use team scoring trends and recent results.
  • Compare odds across bookmakers for the same score.

Manage risk

  • Stake smaller amounts because outcomes are harder to hit.
  • Avoid low information leagues with unpredictable scores.

FAQ

Why are correct score odds so high?

Predicting an exact score is difficult, so the probability is low.

Can I cover multiple scores?

Yes, but it reduces potential profit because you split your stake.

Is correct score better for low scoring leagues?

It can be, but always compare odds and recent scoring patterns.
